Mordane

Septa Mordane is the Septa at Winterfell. She serves as tutor to the Stark girls, Sansa and Arya.

Her primary duties involve teaching the two girls the gentle arts and turning them into noblewomen. She finds Sansa to be a delightful student, but is often exasperated with Aryaâ€™s antics and rebellion.
