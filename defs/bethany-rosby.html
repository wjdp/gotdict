Bethany Rosby

Lady Bethany Rosby was the sixth spouse of Lord Walder Frey. She is deceased. Although House Rosby is not known for their robustness, Bethany was healthy enough to have many children, one each year she was married to Walder; Perwyn, Benfrey, Willamen, Olyvar and Roslin Frey.
