Owen

There are five Owen’s:

Owen Inchfield: A Knight with Renly Baratheon

Owen Merryweather : A Former Hand of the King to Aerys II

Owen Norrey : A Member of House Norrey

Owen the Oaf : A Night’s Watch Steward

Owen (Brother to Maribald) : A former Septon
