The First Blackfyre Rebellion ( First Blackfyre Rebellion, The Blackfyre Rebellion, Blackfyre Rebellion )

The First Blackfyre Rebellion, originally called the Blackfyre Rebellion, took place in 196 AC developed out of a dynastic dispute involving the children of King Aegon IV Targaryen. The conflict was between House Targaryen and the pretenders of House Blackfyre and resulted in open civil war that divided the Seven Kingdoms over the Iron Throne.
