Jeyne Farman

Jeyne Farman is a member of House Farman. She is the sister of Lord Sebaston Farman, the Lord of Fair Isle and the wife of Ser Gareth Clifton. They have many children. She was fat in her youth. Jeyne was a childhood friend of Cersei Lannister. She accompanied Cersei and Melara Hetherspoon into the tent of Maggy the Frog to hear the futures told.
