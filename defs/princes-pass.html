Prince's Pass

The Prince’s Pass is one of the principal overland routes from the Stormlands to Dorne. It is located in the Red Mountains of Dorne. The Prince’s Pass descends from the mountains into the deep sands of Dorne. The head of House Fowler acts as Warden of the Prince’s Pass.
