Ben Bones

Ben Bones, is a kennelmaster at the the Dreadfort sworn to House Bolton. He is considered one of Ramsay Bolton’s chosen few which are known as the “Bastard Boys”. He is an old man. He is in charge of Ramsay’s pack of feral dogs that Ramsay use to hunt young women. He likes Ramsay’s dogs better than their master. It is said he was instructed by Ramsay to train the dogs to kill wolves.
