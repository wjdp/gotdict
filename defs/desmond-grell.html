Desmond Grell

Ser Desmond Grell is the master-at-arms at Riverrun. Ser Desmond has spent the better part of his life in service to House Tully. He has a large belly. Desmond was born of House Grell one of the minor houses of the riverlands. He serves as squire, Knight and eventually master-at-arms at Hoster Tully service.
