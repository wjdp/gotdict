Haldon

Haldon, called Haldon Halfmaester, is a healer and the tutor, He studied at the Citadel but did not earn enough links to become a maester. He had the largest cabin on the Shy Maid, its walls were lined with bookshelves, bins for old parchments and scrolls and racks of ointments, herbs and potions. It was furnished a bunk, writing desk, a chair a stool, and a cyvasse table. He is clean shaven with an lined ascetic face, and wears his hair pulled back and tied in a knot behind his head. He has cool grey eyes.
