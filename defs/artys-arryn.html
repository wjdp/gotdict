Artys Arryn ( Artys )

Ser Artys Arryn, also known as the Winged Knight, is a legendary Andal leader who crossed the sea from the hills of Andalos on the eastern continent. He became the first King of Mountain and Vale and founded House Arryn. According to legend Ser Artys was an Andal leader who set out from Essos to conquer Westeros and spread the Faith of the Seven. He commanded the forces that drove the the First Men and the Children of the Forest out of the Vale of Arryn.
