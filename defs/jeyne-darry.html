Jeyne Darry

Jeyne Darry is the wife of Ser Cleos Frey, the eldest son of Genna Lannister and Lord Emmon Frey. They have two sons: Tywin “Ty” and Willem. Jeyne’s father was the lord of House Darry. She is the younger sister of Mariya Darry.
