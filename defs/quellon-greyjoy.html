Quellon Greyjoy

Lord Quellon Greyjoy was the Lord of the Iron Islands and Lord of Pyke. Quellon had three wives and nine sons, of which only four survived to adulthood.

He is the father of Balon, Euron, Victarion and Aeron. He died at sea. passing the title of head of the Greyjoys and King of the Iron Islands, to his son Balon by birthright.
